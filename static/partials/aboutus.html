About Smart Visitor System

Our Vision

Smart Visitor System is an IBM Sponsored web application for creating an intuitive and self-contained online experience for the Computer Engineering Department at San Jose State University. Powered by IBMâ€™s Watson to provide cognitive computing, this application features natural language Q&A, assistive document preparation, and user accounts to address the most common issues that the front desk faces.

IBM Watson Q&A

Allows students to ask any questions regarding the major, department, and university directly to Smart Visitor System in natural language to have an accurate answer returned. This allows students to avoid visits to the computer engineering for the purpose of asking simple questions.

Assistive Document Preparation

Allows students to upload documents directly to SVS for a faculty member to prescreen and make comments on. Purpose is to allow students to confirm that a document is properly filled out before printing out a hard copy to turn in.

User Accounts

Allows students to access Smart Visitor System feature through their San Jose State University's single sign on account. Store previous asked questions, data, and documents directly on Smart Visitor System.
